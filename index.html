Welcome to AAU Ekpoma Kofa

The Student Information and Registration Portal of

Ambrose Alli University, Ekpoma, Nigeria

Kofa is part of the West African eUniversity Project (WAeUP). It is a powerful and comprehensive Student Management System which serves as the single access point to educational resources and information for all AAUE students.

Kofa controls application and student registration processes and provides comprehensive and transparent information about study progress.

⚠ Please Note: Candidates who have not accepted their admission on the JAMB CAPS page will not have their Student ID and password created on the portal.

⚠ Scam Alert — University Disclaimer

The attention of the University has been drawn to an imposter who is using a new Google messaging service available on Google Search Engine to defraud unsuspecting visitors when they search for Ambrose Alli University website on the internet.

This messaging service is currently being operated by a yet-to-be-identified fraudster. He/she poses as support staff for payments on the University alumni and undergraduate portals.

You are hereby advised not to initiate any conversation with this individual, as this service is not managed by the University or any of her authorized technology vendors.

Please be warned!
